About 3MT®

"Three Minute Thesis®: Innovation Challenges" is the exciting research campaign conducted by Engineering Graduate Student Association of Chulalongkorn University. Developed by The University of Queensland (UQ), the exercise cultivates students’ academic, presentation, and research communication skills. The competition supports their capacity to effectively explain their research in three minutes, in a language appropriate to a non-specialist audience.

Eligibility

Currently active Master or Doctorate candidates of Chulalongkorn University and have a clearly thesis title that related to Innovation or Contributing to innovative development can be applied to this program. Easy?

Rules

We have some key rules for this compettition.

Frist, we need only single page of PowerPoint/Keynote presentation with 16:9 ratio format. No animation, no transition, or ANY movement in this slide.

Second, you have only three minutes to present "this" thing. If you're exceeding 3 minutes you will disqualified for this compettition.

Third, spoken word please. No poem, no raps or song.

Fourth, no media or additional props (You want to present with that doll? Seriously!?).
